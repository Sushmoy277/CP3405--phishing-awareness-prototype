Phishing Awareness Lesson


1. What is Phishing?
Phishing is when scammers send fake emails or messages pretending to be someone you trust. They try to trick you into clicking unsafe links or giving personal information.


2. Signs of Phishing Emails:
- Strange or misspelled sender address
- Spelling mistakes
- Unexpected attachments or links
- Messages full of pressure or urgency


3. How to Stay Safe:
- Do not click unknown links
- Check the sender’s address
- Report suspicious emails to IT
- Never share your password


End of Lesson.
